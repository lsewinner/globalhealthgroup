Who we are

We sit at the centre of the private medical value chain — where capital meets care. As underwriter, we build sustainable portfolios for our capacity partners. As administrator, we stand beside every member at the moment of need.

High-end medical insurance is a promise measured in outcomes, not policy wording. Our dual role as MGU and TPA means the team that designs and prices the product is the same team accountable for the claim, the hospital admission and the member's experience — a single line of responsibility from risk to recovery.

MGU

Managing General Underwriter

We hold delegated underwriting authority on behalf of leading insurance and reinsurance partners, building profitable high-end medical portfolios with discipline and transparency.

  • Individual & group risk selection and medical underwriting

  • Reinsurance structuring and programme placement
  • Regulatory & compliance support across markets
